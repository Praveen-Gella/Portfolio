Capstone · Machine Learning

Employee Attrition Prediction

Problem
Most HR teams find out someone's leaving when they hand in notice.
Method
Cleaned and encoded an HR dataset, then trained a logistic regression model in Python on an 80/20 split to classify attrition risk.
Outcome
86.05% accuracy. Overtime, frequent travel, and years since last promotion came out as the strongest predictors of who leaves.
Tools
Python · scikit-learn · Matplotlib

Capstone · Power BI

Workforce Performance Analysis

Problem
Performance, education, and experience data weren't connected to show what actually drives output.
Method
Modeled 614 employee records in Power BI and wrote DAX measures to isolate the effect of experience, education, and Python skills on performance.
Outcome
Found experience and education both lift performance — and that only 35% of employees have Python skills, a clear upskilling gap.
Tools
Power BI · DAX · Power Query

Capstone · Power BI

Recruitment Funnel Optimization

Problem
No visibility into where the hiring funnel was slow, expensive, or losing candidates.
Method
Modeled 1,000 candidate records end-to-end — application through joining — with DAX measures for cost per hire, time to hire, and offer-acceptance rate.
Outcome
328 hires, a 0.93 offer-acceptance rate, and ₹8.36K average cost per hire — with referrals and LinkedIn outperforming job boards.
Tools
Power BI · DAX

Capstone · Power BI

Training & Development Impact

Problem
Training budgets got renewed on habit, with no evidence they actually lifted performance.
Method
Compared pre- and post-training performance across 5,000 employees, tying training hours and cost to a measurable ROI.
Outcome
An 11% performance lift after training and a 1.32 average ROI — Soft Skills Workshops delivered the strongest return.
